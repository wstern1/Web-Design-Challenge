Humidity Vs. Proximity to Equator

Our second comparison is that of cities in relation to their distance to the equator against their recorded Humidity.

As of 1/5/17, one could surmise that there isn't much of a trend for the humidity of a city in relation to its distance to the equator. Humidity can depend on a variety of other factors, such as proximity to the ocean, and whether the city is on an island or in a rainforest, for example.
